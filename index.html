About Us

Ethical HCK Community is a part of the Herald DevCorps Communities at Herald College Kathmandu, established on September 9, 2021. As a cybersecurity-focused community, we study current cyber threats and educate others on how to avoid them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students and professionals to understand and defend against modern cyber threats. We aim to build a community of ethical hackers who are skilled, knowledgeable, and committed to making the digital world safer for everyone.
